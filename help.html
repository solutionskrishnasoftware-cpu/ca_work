Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I set up my CA Dashboard account?

After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Once verified, log in and complete your profile setup. We recommend starting with the Settings section to configure your company details, tax rates, and invoice templates. The onboarding wizard will guide you through the essential setup steps.

Can I import data from my existing accounting software?

Yes, CA Dashboard supports importing client data, invoices, and transactions from QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and other popular accounting platforms. Go to Settings > Data Management > Import to upload CSV files or connect directly through our API integration.

How many clients can I add to my account?

The number of clients depends on your subscription plan. The Professional plan allows up to 50 clients, while the Firm plan offers unlimited clients. You can upgrade your plan at any time from the Billing section in Settings.

Invoicing & Billing

How do I create and send an invoice to a client?

Navigate to the Invoices section and click "Create Invoice". Select your client, add line items for services or products, set the due date, and apply any taxes or discounts. You can preview the invoice before sending. Click "Send Invoice" to email it directly to your client, or "Save as Draft" to work on it later.

Can I set up recurring invoices?

Yes, you can set up recurring invoices for regular clients. When creating an invoice, toggle the "Recurring" option and set the frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annually). The system will automatically generate and send invoices according to your schedule.

What payment methods do you support?

CA Dashboard integrates with multiple payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, and Square. Clients can pay invoices via credit card, bank transfer, or digital wallets. You can enable payment methods in Settings > Payment Processing.

Tax Management

How do I set up automatic tax calculations?

Go to Settings > Tax Configuration to set up tax rules. You can create different tax rates for various services, products, or client locations. The system will automatically apply the correct tax rates based on your rules when creating invoices or recording expenses.

Can I track tax deadlines for multiple clients?

Yes, the Tax Filing section includes a comprehensive deadline tracker. You can set tax deadlines for each client individually, and the system will send you reminders as due dates approach. Color-coded alerts indicate urgency levels.

Does CA Dashboard support GST, VAT, and sales tax?

Yes, the platform supports all major tax types including GST, VAT, sales tax, and income tax. You can configure multiple tax jurisdictions and rules to ensure compliance with local regulations in different countries and states.

Security & Privacy

What security measures are in place to protect client data?

CA Dashboard uses bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission and storage. We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing. Two-factor authentication is available for added security. All data is stored on secure, compliant servers with 24/7 monitoring.

Is my data backed up regularly?

Yes, we perform automated daily backups of all customer data. Additionally, you can manually export your data at any time from Settings > Data Management > Export. Our backup systems are geographically distributed for redundancy.

How can I enable two-factor authentication?

Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. You can choose to receive verification codes via SMS or use an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy. We highly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced account security.

Billing & Subscription

What happens if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing section in Settings. After cancellation, you'll retain access to your account until the end of your billing period. We recommend exporting your data before the account is fully deactivated. You can reactivate your account within 30 days of cancellation.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features become available immediately. When downgrading, the changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Prorated charges or credits are applied accordingly.

Do you offer discounts for accounting firms?

Yes, we offer special pricing for accounting firms with multiple users. Contact our sales team at sales@cadashboard.com to discuss custom enterprise plans, volume discounts, and white-label options for firms that want to rebrand the platform for their practice.
